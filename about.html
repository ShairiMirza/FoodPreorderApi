About UPTM Cafe Preorder System

Our system allows students to preorder meals conveniently, reducing waiting time and ensuring fresh food delivery on time.

Vision

Our vision is to become the leading pre-order cafe at UPTM. We aim to provide students with a seamless online ordering experience, ensuring meals are ready on time and satisfaction is guaranteed. By leveraging technology, we strive to enhance the campus dining experience for every student.

Mission

  • Provide an easy-to-use online pre-order system.
  • Serve fresh and quality meals to students on time.
  • Enhance student satisfaction by reducing waiting time.
